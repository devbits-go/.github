- `DevBits` is a small social app for sharing short posts, projects, and quick moments. We built it to feel simple, readable, and focused on conversations instead of noise. You can post short updates called bytes, create and manage streams, save things you like, and follow discussions that matter to you.

- The app is designed to be easy to read and quick to use. Posts support Markdown and images, streams help organize projects and ideas, and compact feeds make it easy to browse. There is also a terminal-style view for people who like a more desktop-like experience. Push notifications and deep links help you jump back into conversations quickly.

> [!NOTE]
> We hope you enjoy using DevBits. If something feels off or you have ideas for improving it, feel free to reach out. We read the feedback and are always trying to make the app better.
